✦ NYC Futures Labs — Travel Fund
Field Work · New York City
Travel to NYC for Futures Literacy Labs
🛡️ Health accommodation · Elder + child protection · COVID-conscious practice

When Makēda travels to NYC to facilitate futures labs in schools and community spaces, she works with elders and children — populations who require extra protection from respiratory illness. Using a rental car for the week rather than the subway is not a luxury. It is a health accommodation and a professional necessity: protecting participants from exposure, giving Makēda a quiet mobile workspace to prepare and recover between sessions, and supporting a nervous system that is doing intense relational and facilitation work every day of that trip.
