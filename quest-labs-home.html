The Framework · ABCDEFG

Seven Literacies for a World in Transition

The futures are not missing. They've been confiscated. Decades of deficit-model education trained whole generations to react to crises rather than author alternatives — to manage uncertainty rather than inhabit it with skill.

Quest Labs builds the infrastructure for what comes next. Not retrofitted onto broken systems. Built from scratch, from the ground up, with community wisdom as the foundation.

The ABCDEFG Futures Literacies Framework emerged from 545 contributions by 170 educators across two healing-centered gatherings. It is not a curriculum. It is a living methodology — seven interlocking capacities that, together, form a complete ecology of futures intelligence.

This is shadow work and emergence work. The archaeology of self and the bridge to thriving. Both at once.

The Warmup Suite · Quest Labs Toolkit

The Games Before the Game

Futures literacy is a muscle. These are the warmups — the tools that make the deeper work possible.

01 · Scan
STEEP BINGO
Environmental signal scanning as a participatory bingo card. Players spot real-world change across Social, Technological, Economic, Ecological, and Political domains — activating collective pattern recognition before scenario work begins.
02 · Inhabit
SEL Snapshots & Snippets
Snapshots are future-tense scenario vignettes — short narrative scenes from a possible world. Snippets are artifacts from an imagined past: a ticket stub, a label, a memo. Together they build futures fluency through feeling before analysis.
03 · Map
Madlibs of a Multiverse
A new medium: small language models embedded in participatory fill-in-the-blank structures. Players complete narrative templates that diverge into multiple futures branches — generating a living map of the possible. Not a prediction engine. A possibility garden.
04 · Embody
The Black Menagerie
A speculative card game where players inhabit Afrofuturist, ecological, and postcolonial archetypes to navigate systems-in-transition. Each creature carries distinct knowledge and blindspots — collective sense-making unlocks what no single player can see.
05 · Ground
Portal of Possibilities
The flagship healing-centered futures gathering. A full-day experience combining all tools, facilitated by Makēda, generating collective future visions that become living research. Where the framework was born — and where it keeps evolving.
06 · Synthesize
Participation Zettels
Every contribution — a word cloud entry, a card played, a Miro note — becomes a breadcrumb in the collective zettelkasten. Your participation is recorded, remembered, rewarded. The map grows richer the more people show up.
