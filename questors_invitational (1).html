The Premise

Somewhere between 2026 and 2032,
there is a threshold.

You don't cross it. You build it — from both sides, simultaneously.

Over 12 weeks, a founding cohort of 8 travelers move through 12 worlds. Each week the world changes: same crew, different physics. You keep what is portable about yourself. You discover what you're building toward. And by the final session, you hold a 2032 Job Description — assembled piece by piece from 12 weeks of morning pages, creative practice, and sandbox sessions.

Not a resume. A living document. Equal parts manifesto, methodology, and spell.

What You Actually Do

The weekly
practice.

📓
Daily
  • → Morning pages — 3 handwritten pages, first thing
  • → Non-negotiable. No rereading. Just write.
  • → ~45 minutes
🎨
Weekly
  • → 90-min live session (recorded to Skool)
  • → One Artist Date — solo creative outing, your choice
  • → One Skool community post
  • → ~5–8 hrs total depending on tier
⚡
Each Session
  • → Arrival ritual: 1-word check-in
  • → World Skin activation: enter the week's world
  • → Main Quest: sandbox, creative work, futures tool
  • → Solo → Dyad → Share (me-we-re-search)
  • → Mad Libs reveal: one new blank unlocked
  • → Closing ritual: name your artifact of the week
🗝️
Artifacts You Collect
  • 🗝️ Keys — beliefs or blocks unlocked
  • 🧭 Maps — frameworks developed or remixed
  • 💌 Dispatches — writing, voice memos, creative objects
  • 🪞 Mirrors — what a dyad partner reflected back
  • 🌱 Seeds — ideas not ready yet, held for growing
