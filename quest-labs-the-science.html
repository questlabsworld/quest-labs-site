ABCDEFG Futures Literacies Framework

© Makēda — All rights reserved. Masters thesis, Freie Universität Berlin, 2026. Doctoral work in progress, Humboldt University Berlin — where W.E.B. Du Bois studied. This is the contribution to the literature.

The gap this fills
Riel Miller's Futures Literacy framework is primarily cognitive-dominant. The ABCDEFG Framework extends FL into the full dimensions of human knowing: affective, behavioral, embodied, dialectical, creative, and generative — not as additions, but as the spine. This is what healing-centered educator professional development actually requires. 545 contributions. 170 educators. 2 gathering cycles. Validated.
A Affective Emotion as data. Feeling as knowing.
B Behavioral Action in uncertainty. Embodied habits.
C Cognitive Sense-making. Mental models. Futures thinking.
D Dialectical Horizons. Digital positionality. Tension-holding.
E Embodied / Experiential Play as pedagogy. Being IN the future.
F Fun / Creative Joy as method. Art as futures practice.
G Generative New infrastructure. Not retrofitting.
A
Affective
Emotional intelligence as a futures competency. Feeling-as-data. Somatic knowing as legitimate epistemology. The body is an instrument, not a distraction.
B
Behavioral
How we actually act when facing uncertainty — not how we think we should act. Patterns. Habits. The gap between espoused theory and theory-in-use.
C
Cognitive
Traditional sense-making, mental models, systems thinking. This is where most FL frameworks stop. ABCDEFG starts here and goes further.
D
Dialectical — yes baby 💅🏾
Futures horizons. Holding opposites as generative. Digital positionality (Stuart Candy connection). Already alive in viola practice, merch, and music. Multiple truths simultaneously. This is the dimension that honors where you're from.
E
Embodied / Experiential
Jake Dunagan's experiential futures methodology made embodied. Play as pedagogy. Being IN the future, not just thinking about it. The game IS the learning. The sandbox IS the research.
F
Fun / Creative
Joy as legitimate futures methodology. Art, music, movement, play — not as engagement tactics but as epistemological modes. Blueprint. Baksana. The viola. This dimension argues that delight IS rigor.
G
Generative
Building new infrastructure rather than retrofitting old systems. Quest Labs itself is G in action. The entire digital suite. The Open Collective. The crew. Building the new game rather than playing the old one better.
∞
CLA as Comic Strips + Madlibs
Inayatullah's four layers taught through comic strips and madlib formats. This IS the pedagogical innovation — academic rigor made accessible to any human. Not dumbing down. Opening up.
Guiding principle Minimum Viable Pedagogy — rigorous, accessible, and no more complex than it needs to be.

Why Humboldt + Du Bois matters
W.E.B. Du Bois studied at Humboldt University Berlin (Friedrich Wilhelm University, as it was then) in the 1890s. He later wrote that his time in Germany freed him from "the racial provincialism of America" and opened a genuinely global epistemology. Makēda's doctoral work at Humboldt — an Afroasian futurist extending futures literacy into healing-centered practice — is in direct lineage with this tradition. This is not a coincidence. It is a calling.
04
Unique Value Proposition

Why does Quest Labs exist and why now? What can niemand deny?

  • 1
    Direct training with the field's originating scholars
    Studied with Miller (UNESCO, 2025), co-facilitated with Miller (2026). Studied with Inayatullah (2025). Studied with Dunagan/IFTF (2025). Stuart Candy spoke in Makēda's class. This is not a reading list. These are relationships. The positionality IS the methodology.
  • 2
    Empirically validated — not proposed
    545 contributions. 170 educators. 300 students. 30 teachers. 20 years. The ABCDEFG Framework is not a hypothesis. It is a finding.
  • 3
    Fills a documented gap in the FL literature
    Miller's FL is cognitive-dominant. Inayatullah's CLA was designed for policy analysts. Dunagan's experiential futures is design-focused. ABCDEFG is the first framework that centers the whole educator: affective, behavioral, cognitive, dialectical, embodied, creative, and generative. The field has been calling for exactly this. Makēda built it.
  • 4
    Afroasian + healing-centered = genuinely new epistemology
    Not "diversity add-ons." A fundamentally different starting point. Makēda was raised in Alaska. Educated at Stanford. Practicing in Berlin. This cross-cultural, cross-racial, cross-continental positionality is the method itself. The field doesn't have this yet.
  • 5
    The tools ARE the science
    Every interactive HTML tool built for Quest Labs — the Black Menagerie, the sandbox sessions, the STEEP Bingo, the CLA tool — is experiential futures methodology made operational. They are not illustrations of theory. They ARE the research instruments. Jake Dunagan's Experiential Futures Ladder in practice, at scale, with an educator community.
  • 6
    The crew is already assembled across four countries
    Germany, South Africa, Canada, USA. Eight people. Multiple disciplines. Organized through Open Collective Europe. Ready to deploy. This is not a solo researcher with a good idea — it is a living collective with the infrastructure to scale.
  • 7
    Play as pedagogy — joy as method — is scientifically defensible
    The F dimension (Fun/Creative) in ABCDEFG is not soft. It cites Dunagan (games), Milojević (feminist eutopia), and two decades of practice showing that delight produces the psychological safety required for genuine futures thinking. Music, dance, viola, Blueprint, Baksana — these are research instruments.

Why this is academically defensible
The F (Fun/Creative) dimension of ABCDEFG argues that joy produces the psychological safety required for genuine futures thinking. Milojević's feminist eutopia requires it. Dunagan's experiential futures assume it. Sardar's call for creativity as the response to postnormal times demands it. Blueprint, Baksana, viola, and summer music with friends are not indulgences. They are field notes.
